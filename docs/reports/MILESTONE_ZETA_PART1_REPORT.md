## Final QA

**Can Boss switch contexts faster?**
Yes. Selecting a Workspace instantly filters the available sessions, tasks, and recommended tools to only what is relevant to that world, eliminating visual clutter.

**Can this reduce mental load?**
Absolutely. By hiding "Career" tasks while Boss is inside the "Jarvis" workspace, the mental overhead of tracking unrelated pending items is removed.

**Can this become a daily habit?**
Yes, because JARVIS actively infers the "Suggested Workspace" from passive digital activity. If Boss opens LinkedIn, the Dashboard gently suggests the "Career" workspace, making the transition seamless and habitual without feeling like manual data entry.
